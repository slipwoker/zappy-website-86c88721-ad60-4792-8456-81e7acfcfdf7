    Professional Acupuncture

    Chinese acupuncture is one of the most ancient and proven methods in alternative medicine. The treatment is based on inserting very thin needles into specific points in the body, with the aim of balancing energy flow (chi) and stimulating the body's natural healing powers.

    Acupuncture is particularly effective for treating chronic pain such as back, neck and shoulder pain, headaches and joint pain. It also helps reduce tension and stress, improve sleep and strengthen the immune system.

    • Relief from chronic pain
    • Reduction of tension and stress
    • Improvement of muscle and joint function
    • Strengthening the immune system
    • Improvement of sleep quality

    Therapeutic Medical Massage

    Medical massage and holistic nutrition are a perfect combination for improving physical and mental health. Our medical massage focuses on treating muscle and joint problems, releasing deep tension and improving blood and lymph circulation.

    Our therapists use advanced techniques such as deep tissue massage, trigger point therapy and soft tissue manipulation. Each treatment is personally tailored to the patient's physical condition and specific needs.

    • Deep muscle tension release
    • Relief from back and neck pain
    • Improvement of flexibility and mobility
    • Reduction of inflammation and swelling
    • Improvement of blood and energy circulation

    Holistic Nutrition and Healthy Lifestyle

    The holistic approach to nutrition views the body, mind and environment as one integrated system. We believe that proper nutrition is one of the important keys to health and wellness. Our nutritional counseling includes a comprehensive examination of eating habits, health status and personal needs.

    We build a personalized menu that takes into account body type, energy needs, allergies and sensitivities, and health goals. Our guidance also includes recommendations for natural nutritional supplements, herbal medicine and lifestyle changes.

    • Personalized nutrition menu
    • Close guidance and ongoing support
    • Natural nutritional supplement recommendations
    • Energy level improvement
    • Immune system strengthening
