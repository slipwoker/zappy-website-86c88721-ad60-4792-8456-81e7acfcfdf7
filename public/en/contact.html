    Frequently Asked Questions

    Are the treatments painful?

    Most of our treatments are not painful. In acupuncture, for example, the needles are very thin and the sensation is very mild. In medical massage, we adjust the pressure intensity to your comfort level.

    How many treatments are required?

    The number of treatments varies according to your individual condition and treatment goals. In the first session, we'll create a personalized treatment plan and assess the recommended number of treatments.

    Are there any side effects?

    Our treatments are natural and safe, and there are usually no side effects. In rare cases, there may be mild feelings of fatigue or sensitivity after treatment, which pass quickly.

    Are the treatments covered by health insurance?

    Some of our treatments are covered by health insurance as part of complementary medicine. We recommend checking with your health insurance provider about coverage terms.
